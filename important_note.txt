First login into  your Gmail account in a browser
Now open below link in the new tab
https://myaccount.google.com/security

In this page do following:

Look for "Signing in to Google"
There turn off both 'Use your phone to sign in' and '2-Step Verification'

Then scroll down and look for "Less secure app access"
There click on  "Turn on access (not recommended)" then a new page will open
On the new page toggle "Allow less secure apps" to turn it ON.
